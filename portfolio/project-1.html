Why analysing workout data is important?

Through actual numbers and charts, athlete achieve their fitness goals more quickly and efficiently.

It can ensure that an athlete is performing an appropriate amount of training to promote muscle hypertrophy and can help identify patterns and trends in training

This can be an important part of a well-designed periodized training program, as athletes can identify when it may be necessary to deload or reduce training volume temporarily to allow for adequate recovery, break through plateaus and prevent overtraining or undertraining.
